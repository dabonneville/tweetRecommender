TweetRecommender
Thanks for taking part in the manual evaluation of our tool 'tweetRecommender'.

In the following we will show recent news articles on various topics. For each article our tool recommends a number of tweets.
Your task is to decide whether a tweet is relevant for the given news article.
You may use the following list as indicators for a relevant tweet:

  • adds information
  • fits to topic
  • confirms article (with another source/without repeating the headline)
  • interesting opinion
  • filled with argument(s)

Not Relevant

Please rate the relevance of the tweets with respect to the given news article
